What is CBM

When is CBM made ?

First, let's speak about what is CBM . CBM is the short form of Club BasketBall M'Asaken it is exacltly located in the "salle couverte de M'Asaken" . It is active within the Tunisian Basketball Federation in the youth categories: Championship and Cup . CBM is made in 2014 . Ramzi Aloulou is the president of the club . Hsan Bouhlal is the vice president .

The members of CBM .

The CBM family is composed of 8 categories boys and girls . BOYS : youngest / school / minimal A .. GIRlS : youngest / school / minimal B / younger / junior .

The problems of CBM

Financial problems . In addition to the problem of not having a place to exercise, so that a little time is divided into many categories, which caused a big problem for this family .
